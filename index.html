Welcome to our page, we are excited that you have come this far and are interested in learning more about us. At "Yellowtex" we are passionate about creating and promoting sustainable fashion, and we are committed to caring for the environment.

  • Creation of ecological fabrics from PLA, a material of plant origin and completely biodegradable.
  • Promote a culture of conscious and responsible consumption in the fashion industry.
  • Work philosophy committed to caring for the environment and seeks to promote the use of eco-friendly fabrics in fashion.

Our mission is to dedicate ourselves to the creation of fabrics from PLA, a fully biodegradable plant-based material. We believe that each one of us has an important role in building a better and fairer world, and that is why we work every day to offer you high-quality products that are respectful of the environment.

Why the production of ecological fabrics from PLA?

Sustainability is an increasingly important issue in the modern world and there are many initiatives that seek to address this challenge effectively. One of them is the production of ecological fabrics from PLA, an innovative material with a positive social impact.

Learn More

Reduce carbon footprint

The fabrics produced are of vegetable origin and biodegradable, which means that their production and decomposition do not generate greenhouse gas emissions.

Positive social impact

Communicate in a clear and accessible way the environmental benefits of eco-fashion with PLA fabrics, such as the reduction of greenhouse gas emissions and the use of biodegradable materials.

Innovation

Innovation in the production of ecological fashion with PLA fabrics can be an opportunity to develop new products and more sustainable business models that create value both for consumers and for society in general.

For every 10kg of PLA, 30m of fabric can be produced

Biodegradability of PLA is 100%
